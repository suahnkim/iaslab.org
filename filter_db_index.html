Filter Database (color)

Filter DB is a database that contains resized and filter-applied jpg images.All the images are resized to an optimal size of Instagram post, i.e. 1080 pixels wide by 000 pixels to 0000 pixels high. A total of 10920 images (5460 color, 5460 b&w) have been uploaded to Instagram and are varies by the number of images in a post.
We applied all filters Instagram is providing on various combination of images. Instagram has 41 filters(1977, Aden, Amar, Ashb, Bran, Broo, Char, Clar, Crem, Dogp, Earl, Ging, Ginz, Hefe, Hele, Huds, Inkw, Juno, Kelv, Lark, Lo-F, Ludw, Mave, Mayf, Moon, Nash, nfit, Perp, Reye, Rise, Sier, Skyl, Slum, Stin, Sutr, Toas, Vale, Vesp, Wald, Will, X-Pr) and we applied filters on single image (820 color, 820 b&w), carousel with 2 images (820 color, 820 b&w), 3 images (1230 color, 1230 b&w), and 6 images (2460 color, 2460 b&w).
